       In 1980, electronic music gained popularity throughout the world, creating a huge variety of musical styles, including some that originated much earlier. These musical styles have now reached their zenith. Rock, Jazz and Pop particularly were styles that were no longer restricted to the halls of elegance, and every young person was able to express themselves through music. In this page, you will be taken back in time, a generation ago, in order to experience the unique romanticism of that time along with the mood of life.
